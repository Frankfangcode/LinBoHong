📝 個人寫作素材庫 (B2-C1)

故事一：挑戰故事 (Basketball Challenge)

S 情境 Stepping onto the basketball court for the first time, I was instantly humiliated by my erratic and misaligned shooting attempts.
C 困難 Witnessing my peers score effortlessly left me profoundly discouraged, but I firmly refused to succumb to this initial frustration.
A 行動 I committed to a grueling routine, meticulously refining my shooting mechanics every day after school despite my aching arms.
R 結果 This unwavering perseverance gradually materialized into tangible progress when I finally executed five flawless shots in a row.
R 反思 This modest victory fundamentally reshaped my mindset, proving that mastery is cultivated through consistent effort and self-belief.
💡 句型亮點：
  • 分詞構句： Stepping onto the basketball court...
  • 被動語態： ...I was instantly humiliated by...
  • 複合句： ...left me profoundly discouraged, but I firmly refused...

故事二：熱情故事 (The Driving Force of an Idol)

S 情境 Seeking a sanctuary from rigorous academic routines, I found an unexpected source of inspiration in following the idol Yuqi.
C 困難 Her captivating performances are characterized by an infectious energy, which starkly contrasts with the exhaustion I sometimes feel.
A 行動 Rather than merely consuming her content for entertainment, I actively observe her relentless work ethic, and I strive to mirror her dedication in my own pursuits.
R 結果 Immersing myself in her journey has equipped me with the emotional resilience needed to confront my daily setbacks.
R 反思 Ultimately, her radiant perseverance serves as an indispensable driving force, propelling my heart forward whenever I face adversity.
💡 句型亮點：
  • 分詞構句： Seeking a sanctuary...
  • 被動語態： Her captivating performances are characterized by...
  • 複合句： ...for entertainment, and I strive to mirror...
