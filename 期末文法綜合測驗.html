PART 1 · MULTIPLE CHOICE

一、單選題 （共 40 分）

每題 5 分，共 8 題 · 選出最正確的答案

單選題（混合五大文法）

1. The old man _______ in this village for over fifty years. He knows every path and every family here.
（提示：seem/appear 推測）
B — seems to have lived
推測的是「過去一直住在這裡」的事件（早於說話時間），所以用 seems to have + p.p.（現在推測更早的過去）。
❌ (D) 錯誤：S. 直接接 seem 時，後面不能再接 that 子句。
2. The little girl fell asleep on the sofa with her favorite doll _______ tightly in her arms.
（提示：with + O + OC）
C — held
受詞 doll 是被「抱著」→ 被動 → 用過去分詞 held。
❌ (A) holding：doll 無法主動抱東西，方向錯誤。
3. If Linda _______ the job interview, she would have gotten her dream job at that company.
（提示：假設語氣）
A — hadn't missed
主要子句有 would have gotten → 與過去相反的假設語氣 → If 子句用 had not + p.p.（had not missed）。
4. _______ by thousands of fans after the concert, the singer smiled and waved at the crowd.
（提示：分詞構句）
A — Surrounded
主詞 singer 是「被」粉絲包圍 → 被動 → 過去分詞 Surrounded 開頭的分詞構句。
❌ (B) Surrounding：singer 不能主動包圍自己。
5. Not until the firefighters arrived _______ the residents that the fire was under control.
（提示：not…until 倒裝）
C — did the residents know
Not until 放句首 → 主要子句倒裝。know 是一般動詞，時態為過去式 → 借 did → did the residents know。
6. My teacher stood at the front of the classroom with her arms _______, waiting for us to be quiet.
（提示：with + O + OC）
D — folded
受詞 arms 是「被折疊（交叉）」的狀態 → 被動/靜態 → 過去分詞 folded（arms folded = 雙臂交叉）。
7. _______ what to say in the difficult situation, Amy took a deep breath and stayed silent.
（提示：分詞構句）
C — Not knowing
否定分詞構句 → Not + V-ing（Not knowing）。Amy 是主動「不知道」→ 主動分詞，not 放在 V-ing 前面。
❌ (B) Knowing not：否定字必須放在分詞前！
8. It seemed that the ancient temple _______ by lightning many years ago. Its roof is still partially destroyed.
（提示：seem/appear 改寫）
C — had been struck
It seemed（過去推測）+ 事件比「推測時間」更早 → that 子句用過去完成式被動：had been struck。
對應的 S + seem 改寫 = The temple seemed to have been struck by lightning.
PART 2 · FILL IN THE BLANK

二、填空題 （共 20 分）

每題 4 分，共 5 題 · 依提示填入正確的動詞形式或片語

填空題

1. If the weather _______ (be) better last weekend, we could have gone hiking in the mountains.
had been
主要子句有 could have gone → 與過去相反 → If 子句用 had + p.p.（had been）。
2. The chef walked around the kitchen with a big pot of soup _______ (boil) on the stove.
boiling
受詞 pot of soup 是「自己在沸騰」→ 主動進行 → V-ing（boiling）。
3. _______ (exhaust) after the long journey, the travelers went straight to bed without having dinner.
Exhausted
主詞 travelers 是「被旅程弄得精疲力竭」→ 被動 → 過去分詞 Exhausted 開頭的分詞構句。
4. Not until the principal announced the results _______ the students _______ (stop) worrying about their grades.
（提示：not…until 倒裝，stop 是一般動詞）
did / stop
Not until 放句首 → 倒裝。stop 是一般動詞，時態為過去式 → 借 did，動詞恢復原形 stop → did the students stop。
5. The scientist appears _______ (make) a major breakthrough in cancer research. The media is reporting it everywhere.
to have made
突破「發生在」推測之前（現在推測更早的事件）→ appears to have + p.p.（to have made）。
PART 3 · SENTENCE REWRITING

三、改寫題 （共 20 分）

每題 5 分，共 4 題 · 依指示改寫，保持語意相同

改寫題

改寫 1. It seems that the old bridge was built over two hundred years ago.
⇒ The old bridge over two hundred years ago.
seems to have been built
建造事件（過去）早於推測（現在）→ seems to have been + p.p.（被動：been built）。
改寫 2. Because she was touched by the performance, Sofia cried tears of joy.
⇒ , Sofia cried tears of joy.
Touched by the performance
Sofia 是「被感動」→ 被動分詞構句 → Touched by the performance（because 省略，being 可省）。
改寫 3. Victor didn't realize he had left his wallet at home until he reached the cashier.
⇒ Not until he reached the cashier he had left his wallet at home.
did Victor realize
realize 是一般動詞，過去式 → Not until 倒裝 → 借 did → did Victor realize。
改寫 4. After I submitted the report, I went home and took a long nap.
⇒ , I went home and took a long nap.
After submitting the report
After 不可省略！（省略後無法判斷先後順序）→ After + V-ing → After submitting the report。
PART 4-1 · CLOZE TEST

四、克漏字（第一篇）— 音樂老師

共四篇，每篇 4 題 · 每題 5 分

READ THE PASSAGE

Mr. Chen is a retired music teacher who devoted his entire life to education. He __31__ to have spent over forty years teaching in a small mountain village school, where resources were scarce and students were few. Whenever he played the piano, he performed with his eyes __32__, fully immersed in the melody. If the government __33__ his school earlier, he might have had access to better instruments and teaching materials. __34__ how challenging the conditions were, he never gave up. Not until he retired __35__ that his students had gone on to become musicians, teachers, and artists all over the country.

克漏字選擇

31. Mr. Chen is _______ to have spent over forty years teaching in a small mountain village school.
C — appears
主詞是 Mr. Chen（現在仍存在），用現在式 appears（或 seems）。後面有 to have spent → 正確搭配。
❌ (A) seemed / (D) appeared：時態為過去式，和後文 is 不一致。
32. He performed with his eyes _______, fully immersed in the melody.
B — closed
with + O + OC：受詞 eyes 是「被閉上」的狀態 → 被動/靜態 → 過去分詞 closed。（eyes closed = 眼睛閉著，固定用法）
33. If the government _______ his school earlier, he might have had access to better instruments.
B — had funded
主要子句有 might have had → 與過去相反的假設語氣 → If 子句用 had + p.p.（had funded）。
34. _______ how challenging the conditions were, he never gave up.
C — Knowing
He 主動「知道」條件有多困難，但仍不放棄 → 主動分詞構句 Knowing（省略 Because/Although，語意為「即使知道」）。
❌ (B) Not knowing：語意變成「不知道有多難」，與後文邏輯相反。
💡 第35題補充：Not until he retired did he realize that his students had gone on to become musicians... （Not until 倒裝，realize 一般動詞過去式 → did he realize）
PART 4-2 · CLOZE TEST

四、克漏字（第二篇）— 探險家

共四篇，每篇 4 題 · 每題 5 分

READ THE PASSAGE · 第二篇

Marco was a young explorer who dreamed of crossing the Amazon rainforest alone. His friends warned him about the dangers, but he __36__ to have made up his mind long before anyone could stop him. He set out on a rainy morning with all his supplies __37__ carefully in a waterproof bag. If he __38__ a detailed map, he would never have found his way back to the base camp. __39__ in the jungle for three weeks, Marco finally emerged with notebooks full of discoveries. Not until he returned to the city __40__ that his journey had changed him forever.

克漏字選擇（第二篇）

36. His friends warned him about the dangers, but he _______ to have made up his mind long before anyone could stop him.
A — seemed
全文描述過去事件，時態為過去式 → seemed。後面接 to have made（比 seemed 更早決定）→ 正確搭配。
❌ (D) is appeared：appear 不用被動語態。
37. He set out on a rainy morning with all his supplies _______ carefully in a waterproof bag.
C — packed
with + O + OC：受詞 supplies 是「被打包」→ 被動 → 過去分詞 packed。
❌ (A) packing：supplies 無法主動打包自己。
38. If he _______ a detailed map, he would never have found his way back to the base camp.
B — hadn't brought
主要子句有 would never have found → 與過去相反假設語氣 → If 子句用 had not + p.p.（hadn't brought）。
❌ (C) If 子句絕對不能用 would！
39. _______ in the jungle for three weeks, Marco finally emerged with notebooks full of discoveries.
A — Having survived
分詞構句中，survive（在叢林存活）發生在 emerged（出現）之前 → 用 Having + p.p.（完成分詞構句）表示動作已完成。
❌ (B) Survived：只有被動才用 p.p. 開頭，Marco 不是被存活的。
💡 第40題補充：Not until he returned to the city did he realize that his journey had changed him forever.（Not until 倒裝，realize 一般動詞 → did he realize）
PART 4-3 · CLOZE TEST

四、克漏字（第三篇）— 發明家

共四篇，每篇 4 題 · 每題 5 分

READ THE PASSAGE · 第三篇

Dr. Rivera was a brilliant inventor who worked in a small laboratory at the edge of town. She __41__ to have been researching clean energy solutions for over a decade. Every morning, she sat at her desk with a cup of coffee __42__ in her hand, reviewing pages of notes. If she __43__ additional funding from the government, she could have completed her research two years earlier. __44__ by one failure after another, she never lost hope. Not until the day she finally succeeded __45__ that her work would one day power entire cities.

克漏字選擇（第三篇）

41. She _______ to have been researching clean energy solutions for over a decade.
A — seemed
全篇時態為過去式 → seemed。to have been researching = 比 seemed 更早且持續的事件，搭配正確。
❌ (B) appears：現在式，與全文過去時態不一致。
42. Every morning, she sat at her desk with a cup of coffee _______ in her hand.
A — warming
with + O + OC：受詞 coffee 是「自己在散發熱氣/主動暖著手」→ 主動進行 → V-ing（warming）。
💡 「a cup of coffee warming in her hand」= 一杯咖啡暖在手心，coffee 是主動散熱的，所以用 V-ing。
43. If she _______ additional funding from the government, she could have completed her research two years earlier.
C — had received
主要子句有 could have completed → 與過去相反假設語氣 → If 子句用 had + p.p.（had received）。
❌ (B) would receive：If 子句絕不能用 would！
44. _______ by one failure after another, she never lost hope.
C — Discouraged
She 是「被」一次次失敗打擊 → 被動分詞構句 → 過去分詞 Discouraged 開頭。
❌ (A) Discouraging：主動，語意變成「她令人沮喪」，方向錯誤。
❌ (D) although 後面要接形容詞或 being + adj，discourage 是動詞原形錯誤。
💡 第45題補充：Not until the day she finally succeeded did she realize that her work would one day power entire cities.（Not until 倒裝，realize 一般動詞 → did she realize）
PART 4-4 · CLOZE TEST

四、克漏字（第四篇）— 運動員

共四篇，每篇 4 題 · 每題 5 分 · 最後一篇加油！

READ THE PASSAGE · 第四篇

Yuki was a competitive swimmer who had dreamed of winning gold at the national championships since childhood. On the morning of the final race, she stood at the starting block with her heart __46__ wildly. Her coach appeared __47__ more nervous than she was. If Yuki __48__ her knee in the semi-finals, she would have broken the national record on that day. __49__ deep breaths to calm herself, she dove into the water the moment the signal sounded. Not until she touched the finish wall __50__ the cheering of the crowd surrounding her.

克漏字選擇（第四篇）

46. She stood at the starting block with her heart _______ wildly.
C — beating
with + O + OC：受詞 heart 是「自己在跳動」→ 主動進行 → V-ing（beating）。
❌ (A) beaten：被打敗，語意不符。heart 不是被動地跳。
47. Her coach appeared _______ more nervous than she was.
B — to be
推測的「緊張狀態」與 appeared 是同一時間（教練當下看起來緊張）→ appeared to be（過去推測同時）。
❌ (A) to have been：用於事件比推測時間更早，此處時間相同。
❌ (D) S 後面接 appear 不能再接 that 子句。
48. If Yuki _______ her knee in the semi-finals, she would have broken the national record on that day.
C — hadn't injured
主要子句有 would have broken → 與過去相反假設語氣 → If 子句用 had not + p.p.（hadn't injured）。
❌ (B) If 子句絕對不能用 would have！這是最常見的錯誤。
49. _______ deep breaths to calm herself, she dove into the water the moment the signal sounded.
B — Taking
Yuki 主動「深呼吸」→ 主動分詞構句 → V-ing（Taking）。
❌ (A) Taken：被動，Yuki 不是被深呼吸的對象。
❌ (D) After + p.p. 只用於被動；主動應為 After taking。
💡 第50題補充：Not until she touched the finish wall did she hear the cheering of the crowd surrounding her.（Not until 倒裝，hear 一般動詞 → did she hear）
