🔥 Part 1：動名詞 (V-ing) vs. 不定詞 (to VR)

1-1 意思大不同的「雙面人動詞」

這些動詞後面接 to VR 和 V-ing 意思完全相反，考試超愛考！

動詞+ to VR（未來 / 還沒做）+ V-ing（過去 / 已經做）
remember記得要去做 ✉️記得已經做過 ✅
forget忘記要去做忘記曾經做過
stop停下來去做另一件事停止正在做的事
try努力嘗試去做試著做做看（實驗性質）
regret遺憾即將做…（常接 tell/inform）後悔做了…
mean打算要…意味著…
💡 記憶秘訣：
to VR → 箭頭指向「未來」→ 還沒做的事
V-ing → 動作進行中的畫面 → 已經發生過的事

🃏 翻牌速記（點擊翻面）

remember to VR點我翻面
記得「要去」做（還沒做）
Remember to lock the door.
remember V-ing點我翻面
記得「曾經」做過
I remember locking the door.
stop to VR點我翻面
停下來「去做」另一件事
He stopped to smoke.
stop V-ing點我翻面
停止「正在做」的事
He stopped smoking.
try to VR點我翻面
努力嘗試去做
I tried to open the jar.
try V-ing點我翻面
試試看做…（看看效果）
Try adding some salt.

1-2 只能接 V-ing 的動詞（必背！）

consider 考慮 enjoy 喜愛 mind 介意 admit 承認 avoid 避免 practice 練習 quit 放棄 risk 冒險 deny 否認 can't help 忍不住 feel like 想要 give up 放棄
📌 口訣記法：「考（consider）完（enjoy）心（mind）裡承（admit）認避（avoid）免練（practice）習，放（quit）棄冒（risk）險否（deny）認到底」

1-3 need / want 的被動用法

主詞是「物」+ need / want + V-ing = to be p.p.
The car needs washing. = The car needs to be washed.
（車子需要被洗 → 車子需要洗）

✏️ 即時測驗：雙面人動詞

1. I'll never forget ______ the Great Wall for the first time. It was amazing!

A to visit
B visiting
C visit
D visited
「It was amazing」表示已經做過了 → forget + V-ing = 忘記做過的事。答案是 (B) visiting。

2. Don't forget ______ the door before you leave.

A to lock
B locking
C lock
D locked
「before you leave」表示事情還沒做 → forget + to VR = 忘記要去做。答案是 (A) to lock。

3. He stopped ______ because his doctor told him to.

A to smoke
B smoking
C smoke
D to smoking
醫生叫他戒菸 → 「停止」抽菸 → stop + V-ing = 停止正在做的事。答案是 (B) smoking。

4. We tried ______ the window, but it was stuck.

A to open
B opening
C open
D opened
窗戶卡住打不開 → 「努力嘗試」去打開 → try + to VR。答案是 (A) to open。

5. The garden needs ______.

A to water
B watering
C water
D watered
主詞是「物」(garden) + need + V-ing = need to be watered（被動）。答案是 (B) watering。

🎯 Part 2：動名詞 (V-ing) 必背句型與陷阱

2-1 ⚠️ 最大陷阱：介系詞 to + V-ing

看到以下片語的 to，是「介系詞」不是「不定詞」→ 後面一定接 V-ing！
片語中文例句
look forward to期待I look forward to hearing from you.
when it comes to談到；說起When it comes to cooking, she's the best.
be used to習慣於He is used to getting up early.
be accustomed to習慣於She is accustomed to living alone.
object to反對They objected to changing the plan.
be opposed to反對We are opposed to building the factory.
devote oneself to致力於She devoted herself to teaching.
with a view to為了要...He studied hard with a view to passing the exam.
⚡ 易混淆比較：
be used to + V-ing = 習慣於做…
used to + VR = 過去常常做…（現在不做了）
be used to + VR = 被用來做…

2-2 動名詞黃金句型

have difficulty / trouble / fun + (in) + V-ing
做...有困難 / 很愉快
I had difficulty (in) finding the place.
prevent / stop / keep / forbid / ban + O + from + V-ing
阻止某人做...
The rain prevented us from going out.
There is no + V-ing = It is impossible to VR
There is no telling what will happen. = It is impossible to tell...
It is no use + V-ing = 做...是沒有用的
It is no use crying over spilt milk.
be worth + V-ing = 值得做...
The movie is worth watching.

✏️ 即時測驗：介系詞 to 陷阱 & 句型

1. I look forward to ______ you at the party.

A see
B seeing
C to see
D seen
look forward to 的 to 是「介系詞」→ 後面接 V-ing。答案是 (B) seeing。

2. He is used to ______ up early every morning.

A getting
B get
C to get
D got
be used to 的 to 是介系詞 → 接 V-ing = 習慣於做…。答案是 (A) getting。

3. The heavy rain prevented the students ______ going on the field trip.

A to
B of
C from
D for
prevent + O + from + V-ing 是固定句型。答案是 (C) from。

4. It is no use ______ about the past.

A worry
B worrying
C to worry
D worried
It is no use + V-ing 是固定句型 = 做…是沒有用的。答案是 (B) worrying。

5. She devoted herself to ______ the rights of children.

A protecting
B protect
C to protect
D protected
devote oneself to 的 to 是介系詞 → 後面接 V-ing。答案是 (A) protecting。

🚀 Part 3：不定詞 (to VR) 的進階變化

3-1 不定詞修飾名詞（不及物動詞 + 介系詞）

不及物動詞當形容詞修飾名詞時，別忘了加介系詞！
✅ I need a pen to write with. （write 是 Vi，需要加 with）
✅ She has no chair to sit on. （sit 是 Vi，需要加 on）
❌ I need a pen to write. ← 少了介系詞！

3-2 表目的 & 結果的不定詞

用途句型例句
表目的in order (not) to + VR
so as (not) to + VR		He left early in order to catch the train.
表結果so + adj/adv + as to VRShe was so kind as to help me.
出乎意料的結果only to + VRHe hurried there, only to find nobody home.

3-3 虛主詞 & 虛受詞：for vs. of

虛主詞公式：It is + adj + for / of + 人 + to VR

用 for：形容詞修飾「事情」的難易度
→ easy, hard, difficult, important, necessary, possible...
It is hard for me to understand.

用 of：形容詞修飾「人」的品格特質
→ kind, nice, foolish, stupid, brave, rude, generous...
It is kind of you to help me.
虛受詞公式：S + find / think / consider / make + it + adj + to VR
I find it difficult to memorize all the rules.
Technology makes it possible to connect with people worldwide.

3-4 seem 的時間差用法

同時發生：S + seem / appear + to VR
He seems to know the answer.（他似乎知道答案）

較早發生：S + seem / appear + to have p.p.
He seems to have known the answer.（他似乎早就知道了）

✏️ 即時測驗：不定詞進階

1. I need a piece of paper to write ______.

A (不需要)
B with
C on
D for
write on paper（在紙上寫），write 是 Vi → 需要加介系詞 on。答案是 (C) on。

2. It is very generous ______ him to donate so much money.

A for
B of
C to
D with
generous（慷慨的）是形容「人」的品格特質 → 用 of。答案是 (B) of。

3. It is important ______ students to develop good study habits.

A for
B of
C to
D with
important（重要的）是形容「事情」的性質 → 用 for。答案是 (A) for。

4. He rushed to the station, only ______ that the train had left.

A finding
B found
C to find
D find
only to VR 表示「出乎意料的結果」= 結果卻發現…。答案是 (C) to find。

5. The teacher seems ______ about the incident already.

A to know
B to have known
C knowing
D known
「already」暗示較早發生 → seem + to have p.p.。答案是 (B) to have known。

📚 Part 4：名詞子句與重要句型

4-1 同位語名詞子句 vs. 關係代名詞 that

同位語名詞子句關係代名詞子句
功能補充說明名詞「內容」修飾/限定名詞
that 後面句子完整（不缺成分）句子不完整（缺主詞或受詞）
that 可否省略通常不省略作受詞時可省略
前面常見名詞fact, news, idea, rumor, belief, suggestion, theory任何名詞
🔍 判斷技巧：看 that 後面的句子完不完整！

✅ The fact that he is honest is known to all.
→ that 後面句子完整（he is honest）→ 同位語

✅ The man that _____ came yesterday is my uncle.
→ that 後面句子缺主詞 → 關係代名詞

4-2 關代與數量詞的結合

數量詞 + of + whom（人）/ which（物）
→ 這是補述用法，一定要有逗號！不可以用 that 或 who！

He has three sons, all of whom are doctors.
She bought ten books, most of which are novels.

常見數量詞：both, all, some, most, many, neither, none, several, each, one...

4-3 忍不住的兩種說法

can't help + V-ing = can't help but + VR
I can't help laughing. = I can't help but laugh.

4-4 假設語氣：是時候該...

It is (high / about) time + that + S + V-ed / should VR
→ 強調某事「早該發生卻還沒做」

It is high time that you studied harder.
It is about time that we left.
= It is about time that we should leave.

4-5 否定字放句首的倒裝句

否定副詞放句首 → 後面要倒裝！
否定副詞：never, seldom, rarely, hardly, barely, scarcely, not until, no sooner...than

倒裝規則：
① 有 be 動詞 → be 動詞移到主詞前面
② 有助動詞 → 助動詞移到主詞前面
③ 一般動詞 → 用 do/does/did + S + VR

Never have I seen such a beautiful sunset.
Seldom does she complain about anything.
Hardly had he arrived when it started raining.

✏️ 即時測驗：名詞子句 & 重要句型

1. The news that the president ______ was shocking. (同位語)

A had resigned
B had resigned it
C who resigned
D which resigned
同位語名詞子句：that 後面需要完整句子。the president had resigned 是完整的。答案是 (A)。

2. He has two daughters, both of ______ are teachers.

A who
B that
C whom
D which
數量詞 + of + whom（人）。有逗號 = 補述用法，不可用 that。答案是 (C) whom。

3. It is high time that you ______ your homework.

A finish
B finished
C finishing
D to finish
It is high time that + S + V-ed（過去式）。答案是 (B) finished。

4. Never ______ such a delicious meal.

A have I eaten
B I have eaten
C I ate
D did I ate
Never 放句首 → 倒裝：助動詞 have 移到主詞 I 前面。答案是 (A) have I eaten。

5. She couldn't help but ______ when she heard the news.

A crying
B cry
C to cry
D cried
can't help but + VR（原形動詞）。注意：can't help + V-ing 才是接動名詞。答案是 (B) cry。

👹 Part 5：三大魔王考點總整理

這三個是最容易扣分的地方，搞懂它們就穩了！

魔王 ① — 介系詞 to vs. 不定詞 to

判斷方法：看 to 前面是什麼！
如果前面是「動詞 / 形容詞 + to」的固定搭配 → 通常是介系詞 to → 接 V-ing
如果 to 是單獨出現在動詞後面表達「目的」→ 通常是不定詞 → 接 VR

🎮 配對遊戲：判斷 to 的身份！

點選左邊片語，再點右邊正確的搭配

look forward to ___
+ VR（原形）
be used to ___
+ V-ing（動名詞）
習慣於
used to ___
+ V-ing（動名詞）
期待

魔王 ② — 雙面人動詞總對決

核心判斷原則：
看語境裡的事情是「還沒做」還是「已經做了」！
✦ 還沒做 → to VR（向前看）
✦ 做過了 → V-ing（回頭看）

魔王 ③ — that 同位語 vs. 關代

唯一判斷標準：that 後面的句子「完不完整」

① 完整 → 同位語名詞子句（補充說明名詞是什麼）
② 不完整（缺主詞/受詞）→ 關係代名詞子句（修飾名詞）

前面的名詞也能幫助判斷：
fact, news, idea, belief, rumor, suggestion → 高機率是同位語！

Q1. I clearly remember ______ the letter yesterday.

A to post
B posting
C post
D posted
yesterday 表示已經做過 → remember + V-ing。答案：(B)

Q2. When it comes to ______ English, practice is the key.

A learning
B learn
C to learn
D learned
when it comes to 的 to 是介系詞 → 接 V-ing。答案：(A)

Q3. It was careless ______ you to make so many mistakes.

A for
B to
C of
D with
careless 是形容「人」的特質 → 用 of。答案：(C)

Q4. Seldom ______ late for school.

A she is
B is she
C she does
D does she is
Seldom 放句首 → 倒裝。有 be 動詞 → is 移到 she 前。答案：(B)

Q5. The rumor that he ______ abroad turned out to be true.

A had gone
B which had gone
C who had gone
D where had gone
rumor 後接同位語 that 子句，that 後面需完整句子。答案：(A)

Q6. Would you mind ______ the window? It's too hot in here.

A to open
B open
C opening
D opened
mind 只能接 V-ing。答案：(C)

Q7. She has five cats, none of ______ are the same color.

A that
B which
C who
D them
數量詞 + of + which（物）。有逗號不能用 that。答案：(B)

Q8. It's about time you ______ a decision.

A made
B make
C making
D to make
It is about time that + S + V-ed（過去式）。答案：(A)

Q9. She worked hard, only ______ that she failed the exam.

A finding
B found
C find
D to find
only to VR 表示出乎意料的結果。答案：(D)

Q10. He objected to ______ treated unfairly.

A be
B being
C been
D to be
object to 的 to 是介系詞 → 接 V-ing，被動 → being p.p.。答案：(B)

Q11. There is no ______ what will happen next.

A tell
B to tell
C telling
D told
There is no + V-ing = It is impossible to VR。答案：(C)

Q12. We find ______ necessary to practice every day.

A it
B that
C this
D what
find + it（虛受詞）+ adj + to VR。答案：(A)

Q13. She needs a house to live ______.

A in
B at
C (不需要)
D on
live in a house → live 是 Vi → 需要加 in。答案：(A)

Q14. I regret ______ you that your application has been rejected.

A telling
B tell
C to tell
D told
regret to tell/inform = 遺憾即將要告訴你…（還沒說出口）。答案：(C)

Q15. The movie is well worth ______.

A to watch
B watching
C watch
D watched
be worth + V-ing 是固定句型。答案：(B)

Q16. The children couldn't help ______ when the clown appeared.

A laughing
B to laugh
C laugh
D laughed
can't help + V-ing = 忍不住做…。答案：(A)

Q17. He seems ______ the truth before we told him.

A to know
B knowing
C known
D to have known
before we told him → 較早發生 → seem + to have p.p.。答案：(D)

Q18. The bad weather kept us ______ going out.

A to
B of
C from
D for
keep + O + from + V-ing = 阻止…。答案：(C)

Q19. This old building needs ______ immediately.

A repair
B repairing
C repaired
D to repair
主詞是「物」+ need + V-ing = need to be repaired（被動）。答案：(B)

Q20. Hardly ______ home when it started to rain.

A I had reached
B had I reached
C did I reached
D I reached
Hardly 放句首 → 倒裝，用過去完成式 had + S + p.p.。搭配 when。答案：(B)
