Section I

章節導讀

Chapter Overview & Teaching Map

核心觀念：分詞是「會動的形容詞」

分詞由動詞變化而來，分為現在分詞 (V-ing) 與 過去分詞 (p.p.)。學生必須在開場就建立兩組對比直覺：

對比軸V-ing（現在分詞）p.p.（過去分詞）
外形動詞 + ing規則：+ ed　／　不規則：依字典
核心意涵主動 / 進行 / 令人……被動 / 完成 / 感到……
例：a ___ leafa falling leaf（正在落下）a fallen leaf（已落下的）
例：情緒The news is surprising.（令人吃驚）I am surprised.（感到吃驚）

學生最常見的四大迷思

迷思 1：「為什麼是 V-ing 不是 p.p.？」
→ 教學重點：永遠先問「這個動作是自己做還是被做？」
迷思 2：「分詞構句的主詞要看誰？」
→ 鐵則：簡化前後主詞要相同。不同就不能簡化（要保留主詞變獨立分詞構句）。
迷思 3：「Having + p.p. 跟 Having been + p.p. 差在哪？」
→ 前者：主動 + 完成。後者：被動 + 完成。
迷思 4：「interesting / interested 怎麼選？」
→ 看「令人」（V-ing）還是「感到」（p.p.），不要死記「人 = ed」。

Section II

課前暖身

Warm-up — Build the Intuition First

在進入課本之前，用三個對比問題建立學生直覺。

i
"a sleeping baby" vs. "a sleeping pill"

都是 sleeping，但前者是「正在睡的嬰兒」（分詞），後者是「用來睡覺的藥」（動名詞）。
提醒：分詞 ≠ 動名詞。

ii
"a falling leaf" vs. "a fallen leaf"

正在落下 vs. 已落下。直接帶出 V-ing（進行）與 p.p.（完成）的差別，這是 p.196 的核心。

iii
"The movie is boring" vs. "I am bored"

都對，但意義不同。引出 p.198「情緒類分詞」的對比。

Point One　·　p.196–199

分詞的妙用

Participles as Modifiers, Subjects, & Complements

(一) 當形容詞 — 修飾名詞

① 放在名詞「前」修飾

V-ing / p.p. + 名詞
a falling leaf 將落中的葉子（V-ing → 主動 / 進行）
a fallen leaf 落葉（p.p. → 已完成）
boiling water 正在沸騰的水
boiled water 煮開過的水
a drowning dog 溺水的狗（還在掙扎）
a drowned dog 溺斃的狗
用「動畫 vs. 定格照」對比：falling 是錄影、fallen 是照片。

② 放在名詞「後」修飾（分詞片語）

本質是形容詞子句的簡化 — 將「關係代名詞 + be 動詞」一起省略。

名詞 + V-ing / p.p. (片語)
The man who is living in that house is a famous director.
↓ 省略 who is
→ The man living in that house is a famous director.
The car that was found under the tree is Mike's.
↓ 省略 that was
→ The car found under the tree is Mike's.
主動進行 → V-ing；被動完成 → p.p.
判斷方式：把名詞和動詞拉出來想：「是車被發現，還是車發現？」→ 車「被」發現 → 用 found (p.p.)。

(二) 分詞當主詞補語 (S + V + SC)

連綴動詞 (be, become, get, appear, seem, feel, look, smell…) 後面接分詞，補充說明主詞的狀態 / 情緒。

V-ing（令人……的）p.p.（感到……的）
事物 / 情境 The topic sounds interesting.
Your plan seems fascinating.		 —
人 / 主詞感受 — He sounds interested in the topic.
They seem fascinated by your plan.

常見情緒對 — 學生必背

V-ing「令人」p.p.「感到」
exciting / amazing / surprisingexcited / amazed / surprised
boring / confusing / tiringbored / confused / tired
frightening / terrifyingfrightened / terrified
embarrassing / exhaustingembarrassed / exhausted
relaxing / fascinatingrelaxed / fascinated

(三) 分詞當受詞補語 (S + V + O + OC)

主詞 + 動詞 + 受詞 + V-ing / p.p.

感官 / 知覺動詞 (see, hear, feel, watch, find, catch…) 後面接受詞 + 分詞補語：

  • V-ing（OC 主動進行中）
  • p.p.（OC 被動 / 已完成）
I saw an armed robber entering the bank. 我看到搶匪正進銀行（OC 是主動）
Mr. Wang caught Brad cheating on the exam. 王老師抓到 Brad 考試作弊（主動）
When we got home, we found the window broken and all the valuable jewelry stolen. 回到家發現窗戶被打破、珠寶被偷（被動）
受詞補語到底用 V-ing 還是 p.p.，就看「OC 跟受詞之間是主動還是被動」。

Point Two　·　p.200–209

分詞構句

Participial Phrases — The Art of Sentence Reduction

分詞構句的本質：把一個長句子的「子句」簡化成分詞片語，讓句子更簡潔。一定要搞清楚來源是哪一種子句，才知道怎麼簡化。

三大簡化來源

①
形容詞子句

關代 + be 動詞一起省略。be 動詞改 V-ing。

②
對等子句

S₁ = S₂ 時，刪除 S₂ 與 and，第二個動詞改 V-ing。

③
副詞子句

S₁ = S₂ 時，刪連接詞、刪主詞、動詞改 V-ing/p.p.

(三) 副詞子句的簡化（最常考！）

句型 1：一般動詞 → V-ing

連接詞 + S + V…, S + V… → V-ing…, S + V…
When the thief saw the policeman, he ran away.
↓ 刪 When，刪 the thief（前後主詞相同），saw → seeing
→ Seeing the policeman, he ran away.

句型 2：完成式 → Having + p.p.（先發生）

連接詞 + S + have + p.p.…, S + V… → Having + p.p.…, S + V…
Because he has failed math twice, he fears this subject above all others.
→ Having failed math twice, he fears this subject above all others.
Having + p.p. = 強調「先做、做完了，才導致主要子句」。表示時間先後。

句型 3：be 動詞 → being（常被省略）

連接詞 + S + be + p.p./adj./N…, S + V… → (Being) + p.p./adj./N…, S + V…
Because the little girl was frightened by the black dog, she burst into tears.
↓ being frightened（被動分詞）
→ (Being) Frightened by the black dog, the little girl burst into tears.
表「原因」的 Being 通常省略；表「狀態」的 being 可保留可省略。
但 before、after 引導的子句若簡化為 being 必須保留：
→ After being bitten by the snake, Vicky lost consciousness.

句型 4：have + been → Having been（被動完成）

S + have been + p.p./adj./N…, S + V… → Having been + p.p./adj./N…
After John had been told not to drive so fast, he slowed down.
→ (Having been) Told not to drive so fast, John slowed down.

句型 5：否定式 → Not + V-ing / p.p.（not 擺第一）

Not + V-ing / Not + having + p.p.
Because he didn't know what to do, he asked his parents for help.
→ Not knowing what to do, he asked his parents for help.
Because Ms. Li was not satisfied with my report, she asked me to rewrite it.
→ Ms. Li (being) not satisfied with my report, she asked me to rewrite it.
= Not having finished my assignment, I was very anxious.

(四) 省略副詞連接詞後會造成語意不清，則保留

大部分連接詞（because, after, when, while…）省略後語意還清楚，所以省略。
但有些連接詞省略會造成混淆，要保留。

連接詞處理方式例
because, after, when, while… 通常省略 (While) Walking on the street, …
before, after 常保留（表時間順序） Before washing his face, Johnny brushed his teeth.
although, though 必須保留（表讓步） Although a little boy, he is quite sophisticated.

Point Three　·　p.210–211

獨立分詞構句

Absolute Construction — When Subjects Differ

當副詞子句的主詞與主要子句的主詞不同時，不能把副詞子句的主詞省略，要保留下來，這就是「獨立分詞構句」。

句型一：副詞子句

副詞連接詞 + S₁ + V…, S₂ + V…　（S₁ ≠ S₂）
→ S₁ + V-ing…, S₂ + V…
When the sun has risen, the farmers start to work.
（前後主詞不同）
↓ 刪連接詞 When，保留 the sun，has → having
→ The sun having risen, the farmers start to work.
（太陽升起後，農夫們開始幹活）

句型二：對等子句

S₁ + V…, and S₂ + V…→S₁ + V…, S₂ + V-ing…
Luke looked sadly at his mom, and tears ran down his cheeks.
→ Luke looked sadly at his mom, tears running down his cheeks.
The girl rode past me on a bike, and her hair blew in the wind.
→ The girl rode past me on a bike, her hair blowing in the wind.

Point Four　·　p.212–213

With 引導附帶動作

With + Object + Complement

用來描述「主要子句的動作伴隨著的細節」（狀態 / 原因 / 動作），補充畫面感。

三種型態

① with + N + 介系詞 (片語)

He is sleeping on the sofa, with the television on. 他躺在沙發上睡覺，電視機開著
The lady stood with her back to me. 那位小姐背對著我站著

② with + N + 形容詞

My dad often speaks with his mouth full. 我爸常常滿嘴食物時講話
I don't like to sleep with the window open. 我不喜歡開著窗睡覺

③ with + N + V-ing（OC 主動）

The girl stared at the final exam, with her mouth watering. 那女孩盯著大餐看，口水直流
Sam is talking to Mr. Chen, with his legs trembling. Sam 正在跟陳老師講話，腳一直發抖

④ with + N + p.p.（OC 被動）

He sat in the corner with his eyes closed. 他雙眼緊閉地坐在角落
Amy is talking to Milton with her arms folded. Amy 雙手交叉跟 Milton 講話
判斷口訣：OC 跟 N 之間，主動 → V-ing，被動 → p.p.
「眼睛被閉上」→ closed；「腳自己抖」→ trembling。

Point Five　·　p.214–215

分詞慣用語

Idiomatic Participial Phrases

這些慣用語的「主詞」不一定要與主要子句一致，是約定俗成的用法。必背 10 個。

慣用語意思例句
generally speaking一般說來 (= in general)Generally speaking, men can run faster than women.
strictly speaking嚴格說來Strictly speaking, this is not a question but a request.
frankly speaking坦白說Frankly speaking, I can't stand your temper.
speaking of / about談到Speaking of Leo, I met his ex-girlfriend yesterday.
judging from / by由……來判斷Judging from his facial expression, he must be nervous.
concerning / regarding有關（= with regard to）Do you have any other opinion regarding this issue?
provided / providing (that)假如；要是（= if）I will let you play games provided that you finish your homework first.
supposing (that)= suppose (that) 假設Supposing you won a lot of money in the lottery, what would you do?
given / considering
given that / considering that		考量到；有鑑於Given his lack of experience, she has done pretty well.
Considering (that) he is only five years old, it is surprising that he knows so many words.
seeing as / that
given as / that		既然；因為（多用於口語）Seeing as your budget is pretty tight, you may as well book this cheap hotel.
