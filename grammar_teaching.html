L1-1
建(賤)命要堅 — 動詞原形句型

核心概念

這四類動詞後面接 that 帶出的名詞子句時，語意上有一個 should（應該）被省略了，所以後面動詞都會變回原形！

S + 建/命/要/堅 動詞 + that + S + (should) + 原形動詞
類別動詞
建議suggest, advise, recommend, propose
命令order, command
要求ask, request, require, demand（語氣弱→強）
堅持insist
否定句型：否定 not 會留在原 v. 前面
It is essential that drivers (should) not drink before they drive.
被動句型：原形 → be + p.p.
The doctor commanded that I (should) be sent to the hospital.
The doctor commanded that I (should) go home immediately.
Peter suggested that Katie (should) go on a diet and work out three times a week.
The school requested that students (should) inform their teachers before taking a sick leave.
練習題組 — L1-1 建命要堅
1Our boss ordered that the next meeting ________ held on a Monday.
2The rule requires that visitors ________ their hat when they enter the temple.
3Some people requested that the outdoor concert ________ owing to the typhoon.
4Ben's father demanded that he ________ up his room at once.
5John's friend recommended that he ________ a more powerful computer.
6Darren's teacher advised that he ________ too much time playing online games.
7The local people insisted that the murderer ________ to prison as soon as possible.
8The doctor advised that my father ________ up smoking for the sake of his health.
9【填空】My English teacher ordered that everyone ________ (be) quiet in class.
10【填空】I insist that you ________ (stay) for dinner.
11【填空】Before the show, the host requested that all smartphones ________ (turn) off.
12【改寫】Tom had better not go to the concert because the exam is coming.
→ Tom's father advised that he ________ (not go) to the concert.
L1-2
強調 Adj. 跟 Adv. 的倒裝句

核心公式

Although/Though + S + V + [Adj./Adv.], S + V...
⇩ 改寫成倒裝
(As) [Adj./Adv.] + as + S + V, S + V...

📌 英文習慣把想要強調的東西放最左邊！

注意：although/though 常跟 still 連用！
Tired as I am, I still don't want to give up.
Although I am tired, I don't want to give up.
→ Tired as I am, I don't want to give up.
→ As tired as I am, I don't want to give up.
Although Katie walked fast, she missed the train.
→ Fast as Katie walked, she missed the train.
→ As fast as Katie walked, she missed the train.
選擇題時，看到「雖然」語意，就想起這個倒裝句型！
練習題組 — L1-2 倒裝句
1________, he still lost the road race.
2Quiet ________ it was, I still didn't feel at peace out in the country.
3________, he is not a good basketball player.
4Slowly ________, I still couldn't understand what the speaker was talking about.
5________, they are not brothers by birth.
6________, he isn't happy with his life.
7Hard ________ Jessica studied, she still failed the math exam.
8【改寫】Though Peter was clever, he couldn't solve the problem.
→ ________ as Peter was, he couldn't solve the problem.
9【改寫】Although he worked hard, he failed.
→ ________ as he worked, he failed.
10【還原】Difficult as the puzzle looked, it was a piece of cake for Jacky.
→ Although the puzzle looked ________, it was a piece of cake for Jacky.
L2-1
假設語氣 — 與現在事實相反

與現在事實相反

用來表達與現在事實不符或難以實現的情況。時態往前推一個，if 子句用過去式，且 Be V. 一律使用 were。

If + S + 過去式動詞(were), S + would/could/should/might + VR
助動詞語意
would會（最常用）
could可以
should應該
might可能
If I had a time machine, I would go back to my childhood.
If Katie were younger, she would quit the job and would go abroad.
If I were you, I would go to bed right now.
條件句 vs 假設語氣比較：
If it rains tomorrow, we will stay at home.（條件句：真實可能）
If I won the lottery, I would stop working.（假設語氣：與現在相反）
練習題組 — L2-1 假設語氣
1If I ________ the time and the money, I'd travel around the world.
2If I ________ the time and the money, I'll travel around the world. (條件句)
3It's a pity that I cannot go to the concert. If I ________ busy, I could go with my friends.
4If you ________ the teacher's advice, you wouldn't fail the math exam.
5If my dad exercised every day, he ________ so overweight now.
6If you ________ an early bird, you could see the sunrise every day.
7If I ________ you, I would not put myself in such an awkward situation.
8【填空】如果我是你，我會現在去睡覺。
If I ________ you, I would go to bed right now.
9【填空】If I ________ (can fly), I would travel around the world for free.
10I ________ (will) visit my best friend Becky more often if she lived closer to me.
L2-2
虛主詞 & 虛受詞 it

虛主詞 It（代替 that 名詞子句）

為了避免名詞子句當主詞太長，句子頭重腳輕，故用虛主詞替代。

That S V... + V + Adj./N.
⇩
It + V + Adj./N. + that S V...
That she was unable to continue her study disappointed Katie.
→ It disappointed Katie that she was unable to continue her study.

虛主詞 It（代替不定詞片語）

To V... + V + Adj./N.
⇩
It + V + Adj./N. + (for sb.) + to V...
To pay attention to teachers in class is important (for students).
→ It is important (for students) to pay attention to teachers in class.

虛受詞 it（代替 that/to VR 當受詞）

consider / think / find / feel 當不完全及物動詞時，受詞太長用 it 替代。

S + find/consider/think/feel + it + Adj./N. + that S V / to VR
Many people find that an eight-year-old boy can be so smart incredible.
→ Many people find it incredible that an eight-year-old boy can be so smart.
練習題組 — L2-2 虛主詞受詞

2I highly recommend this novel; you will ________ it inspiring to read.
3Most people consider ________ necessary to learn English.
4The students found ________ finish a sixty-page report in a week.
5Many teenagers find ________ exciting to play video games with their friends.
6________ is a waste of time ________ for the whole weekend.
7As a care center staff member, Mike considers ________ an honor ________ the residents.
8【改寫】Playing video games for long periods without taking breaks is bad for children.
→ It is ________ for children to play video games for long periods without taking breaks.
9【改寫】Robert thinks that reviewing his notes before an exam is necessary.
→ Robert thinks it ________ (reviewing...) is necessary.

L3-1
重要形容詞與虛主詞 it — important 句型

核心句型

因為某件事情非常重要，"應該"要立刻去完成或執行，用虛主詞強調重要性，真正主詞為 that 帶出來的名詞子句。

語意上有一個 should（應該）被省略了，所以後面動詞都會變回原形！

It is + [重要形容詞] + that + S + (should) + 原形動詞

也可改寫：It is + Adj + (for sb.) + to V...

類別形容詞
必要的necessary, essential
重要的important, crucial, vital, critical（關鍵的）
緊急的urgent（迫切的）, *imperative（重要緊急的）
否定句型：否定 not 會留在原 v. 前面
It is essential that drivers (should) not drink before they drive.
被動句型：原形 → be + p.p.
It is urgent that the patient (should) be sent to the hospital.
It is vital that students (should) be attentive in class.
It is necessary that everybody (should) be informed of the news.
It is essential that everyone (should) study hard before the exam.
→ It is essential (for everyone) to study hard before the exam.
練習題組 — L3-1 重要形容詞

L3-2
名詞子句 — 間接問句 Review

名詞子句是什麼？

名詞子句（Noun Clauses）是依附在主句中執行名詞功能的子句。名詞子句可以用 that、疑問詞（question words）或 whether / if 引導。

當受詞

I don't know what time it is.

當主詞

What you told me is important.

當補語

The problem is when you will come.

練習題組 — L3-2 間接問句綜合
1Luke is worrying ________ his two cats will get along.
2________ the necklace Anne bought in the market is genuine remains unknown.
3If you are wondering ________, you can always check out the instruction manual.
4Darren is very curious about ________.
5When I asked Amy if ________ to the party, she didn't answer but just walked away.
6Even with the most advanced technology, scientists still can't predict ________.
7【合併句子】Who is the girl in a pink dress? / I want to know.
→ I want to know ________ the girl in a pink dress ________.
...
8【合併句子】I wonder that. / Did you study last night?
→ I wonder ________ you studied last night.
L3-3~5
WH-word 間接問句 ／ whether/if ／ WH-word 簡化

WH-word 間接問句（L3-3）

直接問句變成 know 的受詞後，既然不再是問句，be 動詞就不需要倒裝。請注意標點符號改為句點。

WH-word + S + V（陳述句語序）
Who are they? → Tell me who they are.
How should I do it? → I don't know how I should do it.
Where is Katie? → Peter knows where Katie is.

whether/if 間接問句（L3-4）

yes-no 疑問句變成間接問句時，用 if / whether 引導，表示「是否」。

重要：if 引導名詞子句通常不放句首當主詞使用。
whether 常跟 or not 連用，or not 可放在 whether 後面或句尾。
Whether you love me (or not) is important.（當主詞接單數動詞）
Are you okay? → I don't know if/whether you are okay.
Did you study last night? → I wonder if/whether you studied last night.

WH-word 名詞子句簡化成名詞片語（L3-5）

當間接問句的主詞與主要子句的執行動作者相同，且有 can、could、should 語意時，可將 wh-子句簡化成 wh- + to VR。

wh-word + S + can/could/should + V
⇩
wh-word + to V
I don't know what I can do. → I don't know what to do.
Can you tell me who I should ask? → Can you tell me who to ask?
what/who 為代名詞 → 後接動詞
how/when/where 為副詞 → 後面還有受詞/補語
例：I know where to go. / I don't know how to do it.
