STORY 01 Challenge Story — Basketball
精煉版 · 可背誦 ~140 words
When I first began pursuing basketball shooting, I was utterly clueless. My shots consistently fell short or flew too forcefully, and there were moments when the ball didn't even graze the rim. Watching my friends sink shot after shot with ease, I found myself questioning whether I would ever reach their level. Yet I refused to surrender. Every day after school, I remained on the court, repeating the same motion with relentless determination — even when my arms ached and every attempt ended in failure. Gradually, however, something shifted. I learned to align my aim, follow through consistently, and regulate the force behind each shot. One afternoon, I sank five consecutive shots — a moment that meant nothing to anyone passing by, yet felt monumental to me. It was then that I understood: mastery begins not with talent, but with the quiet belief that improvement is possible.
STORY 02 Passion Story — Yuqi
精煉版 · 可背誦 ~145 words
Being a fan of Yuqi has become one of the most profoundly meaningful parts of my life — something I never anticipated when I first discovered her music. What strikes me most about her is not merely her talent, but her relentless work ethic. Every time I watch her perform, I am struck by the way she channels confidence, energy, and raw power into every movement. I remember watching a live clip where she repeated a choreography section dozens of times — utterly undeterred by exhaustion — and thinking: that is the kind of person I want to become. When I watch her videos on YouTube, I feel entirely immersed, as if I were standing in the audience myself. Whether I am catching up on variety shows, listening to her discography, or reading about her dedication, she serves as a constant source of motivation that sustains me through difficult days. Following Yuqi is not merely a hobby — it is a testament to how the right role model can quietly reshape the way you see yourself.

BONUS 背誦建議

第一週：結構記憶

先背 SCAR 四格的中文關鍵字，確保你能用中文流暢說出故事邏輯，再對應英文。

第二週：詞彙替換

把 C1 詞彙表背熟，練習把原文 B2 版本替換成 C1 版本，培養「升級反射」。

第三週：口說流暢化

每天不看稿口述其中一個故事 3 分鐘，錄音後回聽，找出停頓和填充詞。

第四週：套用練習

看到任何作文題目，先問自己：Story 1 還是 Story 2？然後在 10 分鐘內寫出 200 字段落。
