L4-1 · SEEM / APPEAR

似乎 / 看起來 （委婉推論）

表達不確定的推測，避免直接批評或指責

用法 1 seem/appear 當連綴動詞

接形容詞
You seem/appear happy.   → + Adj.
接名詞（+like）
It seemed/appeared like a good idea.   → + like + N.

用法 2 It seems that… ↔ S. + seem(s/ed)… 改寫

公式 A — 推測時間 = 說話當下
It seems that + S. + V. (現在式)   ← →   S. + seems to + 原V.
公式 B — 推測事件比說話更早
It seems that + S. + V. (過去式)   ← →   S. + seems to have + p.p.
中文情境It seems that…S. + seem(s/ed)…
Katie 似乎很累。
（現在推測現在）		 It seems that Katie is tired. Katie seems to be tired.
Katie 昨天看起來很累。
（現在推測過去）		 It seems that Katie was tired yesterday. Katie seems to have been tired.
昨天 Katie 看起來很累。
（過去推測過去）		 It seemed that Katie was tired yesterday. Katie seemed to be tired yesterday.
Katie 之前似乎很累。
（過去推測更早的過去）		 It seemed that Katie had been tired before. Katie seemed to have been tired before.
💡口訣：seem 的時態看「推測的時間」，to be / to have p.p. 看「事件早不早」。同時 → to be；事件更早 → to have + p.p.
⚠️ 常見錯誤：Katie seems that she is tired（X）→ S. 直接接 seem 時後面不能接 that 子句！

L4-1 練習題

【改寫】1. It seems that Sunny left the party alone last night.
= Sunny the party alone last night.
seems to have left
Sunny 昨晚離開（過去），但現在推測 → seems + to have + p.p.
【改寫】2. Tom appeared to have left his homework at home.
= It his homework at home.
appeared that Tom had left
appeared to have + p.p. → It appeared that S. had + p.p.（過去完成式）
【選擇】3. Nick seemed _______ the movie before. He can even say some of the actors' lines.
  1. seeing
  2. to see
  3. he saw
  4. to have seen
D — to have seen
Nick 以前看過（比推測時間更早），所以用 to have + p.p.
【選擇】4. This castle appears _______ by dragons.
  1. to attack
  2. to be attacked
  3. to have attacked
  4. to have been attacked
D — to have been attacked
城堡「被攻擊」（被動）且是過去事件 → to have been + p.p.
【造句】5. 用 seem/appear 造一句描述你昨天下課後的某個觀察（同學、老師、天氣都可以）。
開放式 — 例：The teacher seemed to have been very tired after the exam.
L4-2 · WITH

With 表附帶狀況

伴隨著… — With + O + OC 四種形式

核心公式 With + O + OC

With + 受詞(O) + 受詞補語(OC)
OC 形式使用時機例子
V-ingO 主動進行某動作with a cat sleeping in my arms
p.p.O 被動或動作已完成with the door locked
Adj.補充說明O的狀態with my eyes open
介係詞片語強調位置/地點with the remote control in my hand
💡 判斷口訣：問自己「O 自己在做嗎？」— 是 → V-ing；被做 → p.p.；靜態描述 → Adj

易混淆組 Ving vs. p.p. 辨別訓練

句子O主/被動？正確 OC
The fans cheered with their hands ___hands主動拍 ✅clapping
The robber ran with two officers ___officers被追 ✅chasing 😮
（是警察主動追，所以 V-ing）
The table with words ___ on itwords被刻上 ✅carved
The robber brought in with his hands ___hands被綁 ✅tied
⚠️ 陷阱：The robber ran with two officers chasing — 警察是主動追，所以用 chasing（不是 chased）！看的是「受詞是不是主詞」。

L5-1 · SUBJUNCTIVE MOOD II

假設語氣 Part II （與過去相反）

五穀小麥 — 談那些「如果當初…」的遺憾

核心公式 與過去事實相反

假設子句（If 子句）
If + S. + had + p.p. + ...,
主要子句
S. + would / could / should / might + have + p.p.
💡 兩邊都用「完成式」概念。If 子句 → 過去完成；主要子句 → 情態動詞 + have + p.p.

對照表 條件句 vs. 假設語氣

類型情境公式例句
條件句（現在/未來） 真實可能發生 If + S. V., S. will V. If it rains tomorrow, we will stay home.
假設語氣 I
（與現在相反）		 現在不可能/低可能 If + S. were/V-ed, S. would V. If I were you, I would study harder.
假設語氣 II
（與過去相反）		 過去沒發生的事 If + S. had + p.p., S. would have + p.p. If I had taken his advice, I would not have made the mistake.

L5-1 練習題

【填空】1. If I _______ (know) your boyfriend was cheating, I would have told you.
had known
If 子句 + 與過去相反 → had + p.p.
【填空】2. If Paris _______ (not take) Helen to Troy, the Trojan War _______ (not occur).
hadn't taken / wouldn't have occurred
兩邊都是與過去相反 → If 子句 had + p.p.，主要子句 would have + p.p.
【選擇】3. If we had had a longer vacation, we _______ to more countries.
  1. could travel
  2. can have traveled
  3. will have traveled
  4. could have traveled
D — could have traveled
If 子句有 had had → 與過去相反 → 主要子句 could have + p.p.
【選擇】4. If Oliver had contacted us a few minutes earlier, we _______ and met him in person.
  1. would wait for him
  2. might wait for him
  3. could have waited for him
  4. will have waited for him
C — could have waited for him
與過去相反 → 主要子句 could have + p.p.
【選擇】5. If Joyce _______ caught a cold, she would have taken part in the school's annual singing contest.
  1. does not have
  2. did not have
  3. has not had
  4. had not
D — had not
後面已有 caught（過去分詞），所以前面填 had not → had not caught（與過去相反）
L5-2 · PARTICIPIAL PHRASES

分詞構句

把兩個句子合而為一，讓英文更精練

類型一 省略對等連接詞 and

步驟
① 砍重複主詞   ② 動詞改 V-ing   ③ being 可省略   ④ and 刪掉
原句分詞構句
Katie sat down, and she listened to her student. Katie sat down, listening to her students.
或：Sitting down, Katie listened to her student.
The criminal escaped from the crime scene, and he was chased by two cops. The criminal escaped from the crime scene, (being) chased by two cops.
或：Escaping from the crime scene, the criminal was chased by two cops.
Tom was fired and didn't know what to do. Tom was fired, not knowing what to do.
💡 and 連接兩句主詞相同，任選一句改成分詞形式，加逗號放前或放後都可以。
⚠️ 原本沒有逗號，改成分詞構句後要加逗號！

類型二 從屬連接詞帶出的分詞構句

連接詞類型連接詞說明
時間when, while, before, afterbefore/after 不可省略（順序重要）；when/while 可省
原因as, because, since通常省略（語意清楚）
條件ifIf (being) + p.p. 常見
讓步though, althoughAlthough (being) + Adj/p.p.
原句分詞構句說明
When I get home, I took a shower. When getting home, I took a shower. when 可留可省
Because she didn't know what to do, she cried. Not knowing what to do, she cried. because 省略；否定加 not
Although he is shocked by the news, he remains calm. Although (being) shocked by the news, he remains calm. although 留下，being 可省
⚠️ After 不能省略！ 因為省略後無法分辨事件先後順序。

  1. Influencing
  2. Influenced
  3. Influences
  4. To influence
B — Influenced

  1. Not knowing
  2. He doesn't know
  3. Doesn't know
  4. Not to know

【改寫】3. As I felt tired, I stopped to take a rest.
⇒ , I stopped to take a rest.
Feeling tired

【改寫】4. Although I am sick, I still go to work.
⇒ , I still go to work.
Although (being) sick
although 讓步連接詞保留；am sick → being sick → being 可省略

⇒ , my mom came home.
After finishing my homework

L6-1 · NOT...UNTIL INVERSION

not…until 延伸用法（倒裝）

直到…才 — 否定字移前 → 主動詞倒裝，強調！

基礎複習 not…until 的意思

S. + didn't + V. ... until + 時間/事件
→ 直到…（時間/事件），才…（動作）
例句中文意思
I didn't do my homework until my mom came home.直到媽媽回家，我才做作業。
I didn't go to bed until midnight.直到午夜，我才去睡。

倒裝公式 Not until… 放句首

Not until + 時間/子句 + 倒裝（助動詞移前） + S. + V.
動詞類型原句倒裝句
一般動詞
（借 do/does/did）		 I didn't do my homework until my mom came home. Not until my mom came home did I do my homework.
Be 動詞
（移 Be）		 I was not allowed to go home until I finished the work. Not until I finished the work was I allowed to go home.
助動詞
（移助動詞）		 I will not stop saying "sorry" until you accept my apology. Not until you accept my apology will I stop saying "sorry."
💡 口訣：Not until 放前 → 把助動詞/Be/did 移到主詞前面，其他不動！
⚠️ Not until 子句裡面「不倒裝」！只有後面主要子句才倒裝。

L6-1 練習題

【倒裝改寫】1. She didn't know who her real father was until she was 30.
⇒ Not until she was 30 who her real father was.
did she know
一般動詞 know → 借 did 倒裝 → did she know
【倒裝改寫】2. Many people were not aware of the danger until the media pointed it out.
⇒ Not until the media pointed it out aware of the danger.
were many people
Be 動詞 were → 移到主詞前 → were many people
【選擇】3. Not until the young man climbs up to the mountaintop _______ how great the scenery is.
  1. he will realize
  2. will he realize
  3. he realizes
  4. he has realized
B — will he realize
助動詞 will → 倒裝移前 → will he realize
【選擇】4. Not until we finish our work _______ time to relax.
  1. we will have
  2. we have
  3. have we
  4. will we have
D — will we have
助動詞 will → 倒裝 → will we have
【選擇】5. _______ Mary stormed out did George know that he had made a huge mistake.
  1. After
  2. If
  3. Not until
  4. No longer
C — Not until
後面有倒裝 did George know → 前面一定是 Not until 倒裝結構
